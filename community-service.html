Community Service

Delta Gamma Iota actively participates in community support projects throughout Muncie. Below are two of our most meaningful annual service efforts.

Muncie Animal Shelter Support

Our brothers volunteer regularly at the Muncie Animal Shelter, helping clean facilities, walk dogs, organize supplies, and support staff with daily care needs.

This partnership reflects our commitment to improving the lives of animals in our community while promoting responsible pet care and compassionate service.

Holidays at Gresham

Holidays at Gresham is a drive-through Christmas light experience hosted by Meridian Health Services at the Suzanne Gresham Center. Thousands of families visit each year to enjoy the festive displays.

Delta Gamma Iota volunteers assist with traffic flow, greet families, and help ensure the event runs smoothly. It’s one of our most joyful and impactful annual service projects.
