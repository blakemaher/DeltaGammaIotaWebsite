Community Service

Service to our local community is a core pillar of Delta Gamma Iota. Below is one of our signature annual service events.

Holidays at Gresham

Holidays at Gresham is a cherished annual community service event where our brothers volunteer at the Suzanne Gresham Center's drive-through Christmas light show, hosted by Meridian Health Services.

During this event, members dress in festive Christmas outfits and help guide vehicles safely through the beautifully decorated light displays. Our role is to assist with traffic flow, greet families, and ensure guests enjoy a smooth, memorable holiday experience.

This event allows Delta Gamma Iota to directly support the Muncie community while spreading joy and positivity during the holiday season.
