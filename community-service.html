Community Service

Delta Gamma Iota is committed to serving the Muncie community through hands-on volunteerism. One of our signature annual service traditions is highlighted below.

Holidays at Gresham

Holidays at Gresham is an annual drive-through Christmas light experience hosted by Meridian Health Services at the Suzanne Gresham Center. Thousands of families attend each year to enjoy the festive displays.

Our brothers volunteer by dressing in holiday-themed outfits and assisting with traffic flow, greeting families, and ensuring the event runs smoothly. It is one of our most cherished opportunities to give back and bring joy to the Muncie community.
