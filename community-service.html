Community Service

Delta Gamma Iota is committed to serving the Muncie community through hands-on volunteerism. Below are a few highlights of our chapter's contributions.

Muncie Animal Shelter Volunteer Work

Our active chapter regularly volunteers at the Muncie Animal Shelter, assisting with cleaning, organizing supplies, walking dogs, and supporting day‑to‑day operations.

These visits strengthen our commitment to community engagement and provide essential help to an organization that serves hundreds of animals every year. Brothers enjoy making a direct impact and giving their time to improve the wellbeing of animals in need.

Holidays at Gresham

Holidays at Gresham is an annual drive-through Christmas light experience hosted by Meridian Health Services at the Suzanne Gresham Center. Thousands of families attend each year to enjoy the festive displays.

Our brothers volunteer by dressing in holiday-themed outfits and assisting with traffic flow, greeting families, and ensuring the event runs smoothly. It is one of our most cherished opportunities to give back and bring joy to the Muncie community.
