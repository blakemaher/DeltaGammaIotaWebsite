Community Service

Delta Gamma Iota is committed to serving the Muncie community through meaningful and consistent volunteer work. Below are two highlights of our chapter’s annual service efforts.

Holidays at Gresham

Holidays at Gresham is an annual drive-through Christmas light experience hosted by Meridian Health Services at the Suzanne Gresham Center. Thousands of families attend each year to enjoy the beautiful displays and holiday atmosphere.

Our brothers volunteer by assisting with traffic flow, greeting families, supporting event staff, and contributing to the cheerful atmosphere. It is one of our most cherished long-standing traditions of giving back to the Muncie community.

Muncie Animal Shelter Support

The brothers of Delta Gamma Iota proudly support the Muncie Animal Shelter through ongoing volunteer initiatives. Members participate in shelter clean‑ups, dog walking, play sessions, and event support — helping improve the day‑to‑day comfort and well‑being of the animals.

This service not only strengthens our chapter’s commitment to compassion and community, but also provides much‑needed hands‑on help to a vital local organization.
