Executive Council

The elected leaders responsible for guiding the brotherhood, upholding our values, and ensuring the continued success of Delta Gamma Iota.

President — Dylan Cakora

Oversees chapter operations, leads communication with alumni, and represents the fraternity with professionalism, integrity, and pride.

Vice President — Isaiah Grady

Maintains accountability and structure, assists the President with chapter leadership, and organizes major fraternity events including formal.

Controller — Jordan Dick

Manages fraternity finances, collects dues and donations, oversees scholarships, and ensures the chapter operates with financial responsibility.

Chaplain — William Rouse

Provides emotional and moral support to members, handles conflict resolution, and promotes unity and integrity within the brotherhood.

Secretary — Colin Yoder

Oversees academics, conducts grade checks, helps organize chapter information, and ensures strong academic performance among brothers.
