Executive Council

Meet the elected officers who guide the direction, values, and operations of Delta Gamma Iota.

President

Dylan Cakora

Leader of the fraternity, responsible for alumni board communication, alumni outreach, and upholding the face and reputation of our brotherhood.

Vice President

Isaiah Grady

Holds members accountable, organizes the annual formal, assists the President in chapter matters, and keeps operations focused and on track.

Controller

Jordan Dick

Manages fraternity finances, collects dues and donations, coordinates scholarship processes, and helps orchestrate fundraising events.

William Rouse

Secretary
