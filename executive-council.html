Executive Council

These elected officers uphold the standards, integrity, and lifelong brotherhood of Delta Gamma Iota.

President — Dylan Cakora

The President oversees all chapter operations, maintains alumni relations, and represents the fraternity with dignity, professionalism, and pride.

Vice President — Isaiah Grady

The Vice President ensures accountability, organizes major events, and supports the President in maintaining chapter excellence.

Controller — Jordan Dick

The Controller manages fraternity finances, oversees dues, fundraisers, and donations, and ensures responsible financial stewardship.

Chaplain — William Rouse

The Chaplain provides emotional support, facilitates conflict resolution, promotes unity, and strengthens the moral foundation of the brotherhood.

Secretary — Colin Yoder

The Secretary coordinates academic check-ins, organizes study sessions, conducts grade reviews, and maintains essential chapter documentation.
