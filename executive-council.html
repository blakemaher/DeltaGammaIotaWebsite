Executive Council

These elected officers uphold the standards, integrity, and lifelong brotherhood of Delta Gamma Iota.

President

The President oversees all chapter operations, maintains alumni relations, and represents the fraternity with dignity, professionalism, and pride.

Vice President

The Vice President ensures accountability within the chapter, organizes major events, and supports the President in administrative responsibilities.

Controller

The Controller manages fraternity finances, oversees dues, scholarships, and fundraising initiatives, while ensuring financial transparency and sustainability.

Chaplain

The Chaplain supports the emotional well-being of members, fosters unity, addresses disputes, and promotes moral integrity within the brotherhood.

Secretary

The Secretary manages academic compliance, organizes study tables, oversees grade checks, and maintains essential chapter records and documents.
