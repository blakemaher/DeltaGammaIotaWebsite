Executive Council

Leadership dedicated to guiding the brotherhood with honor, purpose, and tradition.

President — Dylan Cakora

The President serves as the chief representative of Delta Gamma Iota, overseeing fraternity operations, alumni engagement, and the preservation of the fraternity’s values and reputation. The President ensures that all initiatives reflect the excellence and unity of the brotherhood.

Vice President — Isaiah Grady

The Vice President maintains chapter accountability, organizes annual formal events, and supports the President in managing fraternity operations. This role ensures structure, focus, and consistent progress across all fraternal responsibilities.

Controller — Jordan Dick

The Controller manages the financial well-being of the fraternity. Duties include collecting dues, overseeing donations and scholarships, and coordinating fundraising efforts to support chapter success.

Chaplain — William Rouse

The Chaplain supports the emotional, mental, and spiritual well-being of the brotherhood. This role guides conflict resolution, promotes unity, and strengthens the fraternal bond through mentorship and encouragement.

Secretary — Colin Yoder

The Secretary manages academic evaluations, study tables, and grade tracking to uphold the fraternity’s academic excellence. They also maintain records and help coordinate organizational logistics.
