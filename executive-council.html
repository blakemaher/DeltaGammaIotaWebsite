Executive Council

The elected leaders who uphold the standards, values, and vision of Delta Gamma Iota.

President

Dylan Cakora

The President serves as the chief representative of Delta Gamma Iota, overseeing all chapter operations and acting as the primary liaison to the Alumni Board. He upholds the integrity, reputation, and long-term vision of the fraternity while guiding its members with purpose and distinction.

Vice President

Isaiah Grady

The Vice President supports the internal structure of the fraternity by ensuring accountability, discipline, and organizational order. He coordinates major chapter functions such as formal, assists the President in all administrative matters, and maintains focus on meaningful chapter progress.

Controller

Jordan Dick

The Controller manages the financial health of the fraternity by overseeing all dues, donations, and scholarship funds. He maintains transparent records, plans financial strategies, and supports philanthropic and fundraising efforts essential to chapter sustainability.

Chaplain

William Rouse

The Chaplain strengthens fraternity brotherhood through emotional, moral, and spiritual support. He facilitates mental health outreach, guides members through conflict resolution, and promotes unity and character development rooted in fraternity values and respect.

Secretary

Colin Yoder

The Secretary upholds the academic excellence of Delta Gamma Iota by conducting grade checks, managing academic support initiatives, keeping records, and organizing essential fraternity documentation to ensure operational clarity and success.
