Philanthropy

Delta Gamma Iota proudly supports community upliftment through impactful service, charitable fundraising, and events that strengthen the Muncie community.

Animal Rescue Fund (ARF)

Annual Fall Event · Pelt-a-Delt

Each fall, Delta Gamma Iota partners with the Animal Rescue Fund of Muncie to support rescued animals in need. Our signature “Pelt-a-Delt” fundraiser challenges students to throw water balloons at volunteer brothers — all to raise money for a meaningful cause.

Veterans Affairs (VA)

Winter Event · Pie-A-DGI

Pie-A-DGI is an upbeat, spirited charity event where participants “pie” a brother to raise money for local veterans. This long-standing tradition helps support those who have served our country while creating memorable engagement on campus.

Muncie Mission

Winter Semester Event · Hot Cocoa for the Homeless

Our brothers serve warm hot cocoa in exchange for donations benefiting the Muncie Mission. This winter tradition supports individuals facing homelessness by providing resources, warmth, and compassion during the coldest months of the year.
