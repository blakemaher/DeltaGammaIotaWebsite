Philanthropy

Every year, Delta Gamma Iota proudly supports community organizations through intentional service, charitable fundraising, and events that strengthen the Muncie community.

Animal Rescue Fund (ARF)

Annual Fall Event · Pelt-a-Delt

Each fall, Delta Gamma Iota partners with the Animal Rescue Fund of Muncie to support animals in need. Our popular “Pelt-a-Delt” event lets students throw wet sponges at volunteer brothers to raise money for rescued pets. A fun, high-energy tradition for a meaningful cause.

Muncie Mission

Winter Semester Event · Hot Cocoa for the Homeless

During the winter months, we host Hot Cocoa for the Homeless—offering warm drinks in exchange for donations. All proceeds directly support individuals facing hardship through the Muncie Mission organization.

Veterans Affairs (VA)

Winter Event · Pie-A-DGI

One of our most anticipated yearly traditions, Pie-A-DGI allows students and supporters to “pie” a fraternity brother to help raise funds for veterans’ charities. Lighthearted, spirited, and deeply meaningful—an event that always draws a crowd and supports a critical cause.
