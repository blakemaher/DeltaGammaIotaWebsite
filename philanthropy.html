Philanthropy

Delta Gamma Iota is committed to serving the Muncie community with humility, compassion, and purpose. Our brotherhood proudly participates in annual philanthropic events benefiting organizations that make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

Animal Rescue Fund of Muncie (ARF)

Annual Fall Philanthropy: Pelt-a-Delt

Each fall semester, during the first weeks of school, Delta Gamma Iota partners with the Animal Rescue Fund of Muncie to raise money and supplies that directly support animals in need. Our signature event, Pelt-a-Delt, invites students and members of the community to “throw” soft items—water balloons, shaving cream, or similar harmless materials—at volunteer brothers to raise funds in a fun, engaging way.

This long-standing tradition not only raises important resources but also builds community spirit and awareness for one of Muncie’s most beloved nonprofit organizations.

Muncie Mission

Annual Winter Philanthropy: Hot Cocoa for the Homeless

During the winter and spring semester, the brothers of Delta Gamma Iota host Hot Cocoa for the Homeless, an outreach initiative raising funds and awareness for the Muncie Mission. At this event, we provide hot beverages on campus in exchange for donations, directing 100% of funds raised toward supporting individuals experiencing homelessness in our community.

This event not only warms the hands of participants—it helps warm the hearts of those in need during the coldest months of the year.

Veterans Affairs (VA)

Annual Winter Event: Pie-A-DGI

One of our most spirited winter traditions, Pie-A-DGI raises funds for Veterans Affairs organizations through a light-hearted competition. Students and supporters donate for the chance to “pie” a DGI brother—creating a humorous, memorable event for an honorable cause.

Through this event, we show appreciation for those who have served our country and ensure that our philanthropic mission extends to veterans in the Muncie community.
