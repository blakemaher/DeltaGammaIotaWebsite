Philanthropy

Delta Gamma Iota proudly supports community upliftment through impactful service, charitable fundraising, and events that strengthen the Muncie community.

Animal Rescue Fund (ARF)

Annual Fall Event · Pelt-a-Delt

Every fall, Delta Gamma Iota partners with the Animal Rescue Fund of Muncie to support animals in need. Our signature “Pelt-a-Delt” event allows students to throw water balloons at volunteer brothers — all to raise funding for rescued pets.

Veterans Affairs (VA)

Winter Event · Pie-A-DGI

Pie-A-DGI is one of our most spirited traditions, inviting the community to “pie” a brother in support of local veterans. This event raises funding for those who have bravely served our country while bringing excitement and energy to campus.

Muncie Mission

Winter Semester Event · Hot Cocoa for the Homeless

Each winter, our brothers serve warm hot cocoa in exchange for donations to support the Muncie Mission and individuals facing homelessness. This tradition helps provide resources, comfort, and compassion during the coldest months of the year.
